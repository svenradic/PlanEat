Welcome to PlanEat!

PlanEat is your smart app for easy meal planning, recipe management, and shopping list creation – all in one place.

🥗 Create Your Own Recipes Enter your favorite recipes with details like ingredients, instructions, and nutritional values. 🗓️ Plan Meals for the Whole Week Choose recipes and build a meal plan that fits your needs – healthy, fast, and simple. 🛒 Stress-Free Shopping List PlanEat automatically generates a shopping list based on your meal plan – no more forgotten ingredients.
